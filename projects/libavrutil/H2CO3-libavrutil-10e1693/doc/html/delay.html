libavrutil - Delays

libavrutil provides two delay functions with microsecond and millisecond resolution. Microsecond-precision delay is limited to AVR_MAX_DELAY_US microseconds. All functions are considered more precise for longer time intervals.

void avr_delay_us(uint16_t us): blocks for us microseconds by busy waiting. If us is greater than AVR_MAX_DELAY_US, the behaviour is unspecified. May not be precise for time intervals less than 5-20 microseconds (depends on F_CPU).

void avr_delay_ms(uint32_t ms): blocks for ms milliseconds by busy waiting. May be called with any argument (in the range of uint32_t). Should be precise enough even for a 1 ms delay.
