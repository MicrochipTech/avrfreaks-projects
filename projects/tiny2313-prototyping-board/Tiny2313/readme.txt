Tiny2313_2 PCB construction notes

See layout.gif for placement details etc.
A photo is in pcb.jpg.
PCB is double-sided and easily made at home.

Resistors and capacitors are 0805.
Crystal is surface-mount HC/49.
Regulator is 8-lead surface-mount 78L05.
DC connector is Switchcraft RASM712.
Via holes are 0.7 mm
