enum GPIO::Pin

GPIO Pin Constants for the compiled system.

For each port on the target, there will be a corresponding port and pin definition in the form X# where 'X' is the port letter and '#' is the pin number, 0-7.

Examples are GPIO::C3 or GPIO::A0

Enumerator:
NONE 

Do not pass into GPIO::*() functions(!!), but use for your own boundary checks.
